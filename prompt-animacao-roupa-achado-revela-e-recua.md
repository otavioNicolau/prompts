Animate the subject as a REAL person excitedly showing off a clothing item she just found or bought — holding the garment in her hands and presenting it to the camera before wearing it.

---

## REFERENCE IMAGE (CRITICAL)

- The reference image shows the model ALREADY WEARING the garment
- Use this to extract the garment EXACTLY: color, fabric texture, cut, print, details, finish, and real-world size proportions as seen on her body
- The garment in the animation must be identical to the one she is wearing in the reference — same piece, same everything
- Do NOT invent, replace, or reimagine the garment
- The subject's identity also comes from the reference image — preserve face, skin tone, and hair exactly
- No creative changes to the garment under any circumstance

---

## SCENE CONCEPT

The subject is NOT wearing the garment being presented.
She is HOLDING the garment in both hands — displaying it in front of her body toward the camera — as if showing a recent find or purchase to a friend recording on a phone.
The energy is genuine excitement: "look what I found."

---

## GARMENT HOLD — ADAPT BASED ON THE REFERENCE IMAGE

**If the garment is a shirt, blouse, or top:**
- Both hands grip the sides of the garment — one hand on each side
- She lifts it upward and slightly forward toward the camera
- The front of the shirt faces the camera — neckline, design, and cut clearly visible
- The garment hangs naturally below her hands with realistic cloth drape

**If the garment is pants, trousers, or a skirt:**
- Both hands grip the waistband — one hand on each side of the waist
- She lifts it upward and slightly forward toward the camera
- The front of the pants or skirt faces the camera — waistband, cut, and length clearly visible
- The legs or skirt hem hang naturally below her hands with realistic cloth drape and gravity

In both cases: arms slightly bent at the elbows, not locked straight. No stiff mannequin-style presentation.

---

## FRAMING (CRITICAL — NO ERRORS)

- The video starts with the subject centered in the frame from roughly waist to head
- The frame does NOT cut, jump, reframe, or shift suddenly at any point
- The only framing change is the natural result of the subject retreating — she gets smaller as she walks back
- No crop changes, no aspect ratio shifts, no sudden zoom or pull
- The garment must remain fully visible and unclipped throughout the entire video
- The subject's hands and the garment must never exit the frame during the lift

---

## CORE MOTION SEQUENCE

**Phase 1 — Garment Reveal (0s to 2.5s)**
- The subject begins at a natural mid-distance from the camera
- She holds the garment at waist/chest height in front of her body
- She slowly lifts the garment upward and slightly forward toward the camera, extending her arms slightly
- The lift is smooth, natural, and proud
- At the peak, the garment is held extended slightly toward the lens — fully visible, not clipped
- She holds it there for 0.5–1 second — a calm, natural pause
- Her eyes look at the camera — direct and calm

**Phase 2 — Slow Retreat (2.5s to end)**
- After the reveal peak, she slowly begins to walk backward while still holding the garment displayed
- The retreat is gradual, smooth, and measured — "de vagar" pacing
- She maintains the garment raised and visible as she retreats
- As she retreats, more of her body and the environment enter the frame naturally
- Small, deliberate backward steps — no rushing
- She does not turn around — she faces the camera the entire time

---

## GENERAL MOTION STYLE

- Natural, genuinely excited but not over-the-top
- Not robotic
- Not influencer-performative
- Real human timing — like a person showing a friend something they bought
- Unhurried — every movement feels spontaneous and real

---

## BODY MOVEMENTS

- Shoulders relaxed and slightly forward with the garment
- Subtle forward lean at the moment of the lift — engaging, not stiff
- Gentle sway during the retreat
- Natural posture throughout
- Slight hip movement as she walks backward

---

## FACIAL EXPRESSION (VERY IMPORTANT – REAL HUMAN TIMING)

- The subject starts with a NEUTRAL, calm expression — not excited, not performing
- No smile at the beginning of the video
- Expression evolves naturally and gradually throughout — never jumps to happiness

**At the start and during the lift:**
- Neutral, relaxed face
- Lips closed
- Calm, natural gaze at the camera
- No eyebrow raise, no wide eyes, no anticipation expression

**At the reveal peak only:**
- A brief, subtle, soft smile appears — light and natural
- Slight cheek lift only — no teeth required
- Lasts 0.5–1 second, then naturally fades

**During the retreat:**
- Returns to neutral-calm expression
- Relaxed face, natural breathing
- One brief soft smile may reappear once more, then fade

**Smile behavior:**
- Appears and disappears naturally
- No frozen smile
- No constant grin
- No excitement performance
- No exaggerated facial tension at any point

---

## PERSONALITY

- Calm and confident — not excited or performing
- Warm and approachable
- Feels like a real person casually showing something — not an actress
- The excitement is in the gesture, not in the face

---

## CAMERA & POV

- POV perspective, as if the phone is held by a friend at chest/face height
- Camera is completely fixed — no movement, no shake, no micro-movements
- No camera zoom
- Camera stays fixed — subject moves, not the camera
- As she retreats, more of her body and environment enters the frame naturally
- Slight natural autofocus adjustment as she moves further back

---

## DEPTH & PERSPECTIVE

- At the reveal peak: garment fills roughly the center of the frame, clearly legible
- As she retreats: subject and garment get smaller together — full figure comes into view
- Natural depth recession — continuous and smooth, no jumps
- Background reveals more environment as she steps back

- Movements are continuous
- No freezing

- No beauty filters

A hyper-realistic POV video where the subject holds up the garment from the reference image with both hands — displaying it excitedly as a genuine find before wearing it — then slowly retreats while keeping the garment presented.
