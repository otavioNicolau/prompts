Use Image 1 as the BASE avatar image.
Use Image 2 ONLY as the clothing reference.

TASK:
Apply the clothing from Image 2 onto the avatar in Image 1.

---

## IMPORTANT RULES

- Do NOT describe the clothing
- Do NOT invent, redesign, or stylize anything
- Replicate the clothing EXACTLY as shown in Image 2
- Same color, fabric, cut, shape, and proportions
- No creative interpretation

---

## FIT & REALISM (VERY IMPORTANT)

- Clothing must fit naturally on the avatar's body
- Respect real human anatomy
- Natural fabric tension, folds, and stretch
- No floating, clipping, or distortion
- Clothing must look realistically worn, not pasted

---

## LOWER BODY FIT LOGIC (CRITICAL)

**If the lower-body clothing appears fitted or body-hugging in Image 2:**
- Apply a close, snug fit to the avatar
- Fabric follows the leg and hip contours naturally
- Minimal looseness
- Natural compression without exaggeration
- Size appears accurate and proportional to the body

**If the lower-body clothing appears loose, relaxed, or soft-fabric in Image 2:**
- Preserve the loose fit exactly
- Allow natural drape and spacing from the legs
- No tightening
- No reshaping
- Fabric hangs naturally according to gravity

Fit behavior must always follow the visual characteristics of Image 2.
Never force tightness or looseness against the reference.

---

## AVATAR LOCK (CRITICAL)

- Do NOT change face
- Do NOT change body shape or proportions
- Do NOT change skin tone
- Do NOT change hair
- Do NOT change pose or expression
- Do NOT change background
- Do NOT beautify, retouch, or enhance

---

## LIGHTING & SHADOW MATCH

- Match original lighting perfectly
- Preserve light direction and softness
- Natural shadows between clothing and skin
