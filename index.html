## CORE MOTION SEQUENCE

**Phase 1 — Clothing Reveal (0s to 2.5s)**
- The subject begins at a natural mid-distance from the camera
- She reaches down with one hand and lightly pinches or grips a section of her clothing (shirt hem, fabric of the shirt, or the waistband/thigh area of her pants — whichever is more natural given the outfit)
- She gently lifts the fabric slightly away from her body — just enough to present the material and silhouette toward the camera
- The gesture is deliberate but casual, like naturally saying "look at what I'm wearing"
- The lift is smooth and controlled — no sudden yanks, no exaggeration
- She holds the fabric lifted for 0.5–1 second at the peak — a natural pause, not a freeze
- Her eyes remain on the camera throughout

**Phase 2 — Slow Retreat (2.5s to end)**

- She maintains eye contact with the camera throughout the retreat
- As she retreats, the full outfit gradually becomes visible in the frame

- She does not turn around — she faces the camera the entire time
